Alula City

The city of Al-Hijr is also known as Madain Saleh. This is an amazing opportunity to go on a journey through time and trace a distant past, as it is one of the oldest sites in Saudi Arabia. It contains many sites, monuments and architectural artifacts from several eras and civilizations.

Maraya Concert Hall

Maraya Concert Hall was designed to be an artistic piece of work, with its mirrors reflecting the amazing natural beauty elements of AlUla surrounding the building.

Jabal Al-Fil

On soft golden sands, and amid captivating rock formations, Jabal Al-Fil stands as a natural sculpture that embraces the sky.

AlUla Old Town

Books of travelers since the 12th century talk a lot about the old town of AlUla and its importance in the Hijaz region, as it is the largest ancient city after Makkah.

Hijaz Railway Station

AlUla has a rich history of human civilizations that settled in it due to its location on old trade and Hajj roads, therefore AlUla Railway Station is distinguished among the other.

Local Market in Old AlUla

The local market is located in the old town of AlUla, which still preserves its ancient features evident in the construction of mud houses. This traditional market reflects a rich history that extends throughout the town.
