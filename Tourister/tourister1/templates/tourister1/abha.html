Abha City

At an altitude of 2,200 m between the peaks of the Sarawat Mountains, Abha, is ideally located in southwestern region of Saudi Arabia. Its exceptional location allows you to embrace the clouds and wonder around its mountaintops. While consumed with astonishment and amusement, you will feel like time traveling hundreds of years back in time as you stumble upon castles and palaces built from the elements taken from their unspoiled environment.

Al Nasb district

Al Nasb historical district located at the heart, of Abha.

Al-Basta heritage district

Al-Basta district still maintains its ancient legacy as one of the oldest neighbourhoods from which the city of Abha has formed.

Fatimah’s Museum

The minute you lay your eyes on Al-Qatt Al-Aseeri art in Fatimah’s Museum of Aseeri Woman’s Heritage, you will be overwhelmed with its beauty.

Rijal Almaa village

is a village located in the Rijal Almaa province, Asir Region, Saudi Arabia.It is about 50 km west of Abha.
